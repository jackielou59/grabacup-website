ABOUT US

EAT | DRINK | WALK | REST

Taste the welcome as you experience the Café @ Grab a Cup. We serve speciality coffee, from CLO Coffee, delicious cakes and hearty lunches. Whether you want to grab a coffee before a walk around our stunning Estate or you’re planning a catch-up with a friend, we’re ready to be your favourite coffee shop. We welcome well behaved dogs in the lounge area only of the café and all dogs must be kept on leads when walked in the grounds of Waverley Abbey.
